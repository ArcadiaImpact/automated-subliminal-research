You could use the conciseness to make stand-in sentence replacements. That is, you take a piece of text and have the user prompt be something like “you get to ONLY replace this sentence. You cannot change the meaning of the sentence.” and then swap that sentence in for the one in the original prompt. This leaves there being some kind of constraint around expressivity but still allows the full piece of text to be naturalistic. The question, though, is how you pick which sentence or part of the text to modify…
